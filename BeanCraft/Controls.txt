Launch the game using the shortcut, Have fun!

MOVEMENT
      W - move forward
       A - move left
       S - move backward
       D - move right
       Space bar - jump

BLOCK PLACEMENT
       Left click - delete block
       Right click - place block
       Left shift - Change block

BLOCK INDEX
       1st block in hand - Grass
       2nd block in hand - Beanstone
       3rd block in hand - Lima bean ore
       4th block in hand - coffee bean wood
       5th block in hand - S A L
